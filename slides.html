ConfoConnect Smart Slide

ConfoConnect Smart Slide is a service allowing our users to publish slides.

Our aim is to create a virtual place where scientists, students, teachers and nearly everybody else is looking for presentations interesting for them and/or related to their work.

Therefore, we encourage you to publish all your presentations at conferences, seminars, tutorials, schools and also presentations you are using for teaching.

We are also interested to create a historic archive of presentations so if you have very old presentations in a digital form, we would be very happy to publish them and make them accessible to everybody.
